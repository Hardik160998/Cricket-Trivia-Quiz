# Player Stats Quiz: Decoding the Numbers Behind the Legends

Cricket is a game of numbers. A single run or a single wicket can be the difference between immortality and oblivion. For fans who love to dive deep into the scorecards and statistics, the "Player Stats" category is the ultimate testing ground. From career averages to strike rates and milestone centuries, numbers tell the true story of a player's greatness.

### Batting Milestones: Centuries and Beyond
When we talk about stats, the first name that comes to mind is Sachin Tendulkar, with his 100 international centuries. However, modern masters like Virat Kohli are quickly catching up in the ODI format. Did you know that Rohit Sharma is the only player with three double-hundreds in ODIs? Or that Sir Don Bradman's Test average of 99.94 remains the most unbeatable statistic in all of sports?

### Bowling Records: The Art of Wicket-Taking
On the bowling side, Muttiah Muralitharan's 800 Test wickets stand as a mountain too high for most to climb. Shane Warne's leg-spin wizardry and Wasim Akram's swing are reflected in their staggering numbers. In the shorter formats, economy rates and dot-ball percentages have become the new metrics for judging a bowler's impact.

### All-Round Excellence and Fitness
The rise of all-rounders like Jacques Kallis, who scored over 10,000 runs and took over 250 wickets in both Tests and ODIs, shows the evolution of the game. Today, fitness metrics and fielding stats are also under the microscope, with players like Ravindra Jadeja and Ben Stokes setting new benchmarks.
